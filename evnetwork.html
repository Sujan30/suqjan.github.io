Tesla Model S Plaid

The tesla Model S Plaid is now one of the fastest car a person can buy. What makes it special is the fact that it is an electric car. Not only does it go 0-60 in 1.99 seconds (with special tires), it can accomodate up to 5 people. The price? Starts at $123,740. Tesla is changing the car industry, usually if you would want to buy the fastest car (calculated by 0-60 time) you would have to be ready to spend atleast a million dollars. The common arguement is that the model S plaid is an electric vehicle, which is a fair arguement. And it can't really be used as an arguement, because most the people buy a tesla know its an electric vehicle. Further specs of the car:

  • Horse Power: 1,020
  • 0-60mph: 1.99 seconds (with special tires) 2.07 seconds (with no modification)
  • Torque: 1050 lb-ft
  • 0-100 mph: 4.7 seconds
  • top speed: 200mph
